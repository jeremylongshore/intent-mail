# My Projects & Email Context

Copy this file to `context/projects.md` and edit it to describe your world in
plain language. IntentMail's `email-project-context` skill reads it to set up
filing/priority rules, and the daily check-in uses it to understand what
actually matters to you. Write naturally — you do not need rule syntax.

## How I want my daily check-in to behave

- Lead with **clients and money first**, then internal projects, then everything
  else.
- Always call out anything with a **deadline in the next 48 hours**.
- Group newsletters/notifications and summarize them — I rarely need detail.
- Never auto-send anything; draft replies are fine, I'll review and send.
